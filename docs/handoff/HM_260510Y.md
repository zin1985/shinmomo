## Confirmed core findings

1. `$0799` is treated as finalized canonical visibility staging immediately before a masked branch.
2. Append acceptance is branch-exclusive and irreversible for the frame.
3. `append_buffer` behaves as a contiguous visible-prefix structure.
4. `append_count` is authoritative for downstream traversal and appears finalized at slot-loop completion.
5. `$0759` is append-gated execution dispatch only; invisible slots do not dispatch through it.
6. NPC behavior pointers are downstream execution selectors, not visibility authorities.
7. VM/script/event layers remain configuration-only for this path.
8. `$83:F09A`, `$83:F0DB`, and `$83:F0C6` are treated as terminal append-buffer blob runners.
9. OAM pre-registration occurs after append finalization.
10. OAM pressure is downstream-only and cannot restore skipped slots.

## Confirmed graphics findings

1. `execution_frame + cutoff_index` remains the strongest graphics-to-core narrowing pair.
2. Minimal branch-causal CSVs remain useful after aggressive reduction.
3. Graphics-only traces can reconstruct `append_count` proxy from visible summaries.
4. DMA, VRAM, and CGRAM activity are presentation-only for Goal13 purposes.
5. BG tilemap streaming is incremental and spatially bounded.
6. Metatile reconstruction converges deterministically after interrupted streaming.
7. Sprite clusters survive severe OAM fragmentation and transient CHR reassignment.
8. Contribution estimates track logical entities better than transient sprite fragments.
9. BG invalidation and sprite redistribution remain causally orthogonal.
10. Repeated interruption churn does not break bridge synchronization.
